About Myself


I'm Ellen Loretta, a Journalist Enthusiasts with 2 years of experience. Many people say that i great storytelling skills and a nose for the truth as well as an excellent track record as a staff reporter in broadcast group.


With a combination of technical skills and good communication skill, I am able to leverage skills and experience to contribute to high quality journalism.


In my opinion, journalists are not just recorders of events. They are narrative explorers, exploring reality from multiple points of view to present multidimensional truths. More than just ink and paper, perspective journalists are champions of empathy, using words to build bridges of understanding between people. For Me, journalism is not just a profession, but a calling. A commitment to reveal silenced voices, challenge injustice, and provide space for those who are marginalized.
